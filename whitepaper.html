Whitepaper

WaDPro Games is a revolutionary clicker game that immerses players into the vibrant world of virtual farming. By combining the addictive gameplay of clicker games with the excitement of agricultural management, WaDPro Games offers players a unique and rewarding gaming experience. Set in a picturesque countryside setting, players are tasked with building and managing their own virtual farm, from planting and harvesting crops to raising and caring for livestock.


As players progress through the game, they'll encounter various challenges and opportunities, from weather-related events to market fluctuations, providing an immersive and dynamic gameplay experience. With stunning graphics, engaging gameplay mechanics, and a supportive community of players, WaDPro Games sets a new standard for mobile gaming, captivating players of all ages with its charm and depth.

WaDPro Games

WaDPro Games introduces its native utility token, Wage Token (WAGE), which serves as the primary medium of exchange within the game ecosystem.

WAGE tokens can be earned through gameplay or purchased on cryptocurrency exchanges.

As players engage with the game and participate in various activities such as farming, trading, and completing challenges, they have the opportunity to earn WAGE tokens as rewards.

Additionally, a portion of the transaction fees generated within the game is allocated towards a buyback and burn mechanism for WAGE tokens.

This mechanism helps to create scarcity and value appreciation over time, benefiting token holders and contributing to the overall sustainability of the WaDPro Games ecosystem.

Through the use of WAGE tokens, players can unlock exclusive in-game content, participate in community events, and access various premium features, enhancing their overall gaming experience and incentivizing continued engagement.
